About Me

I’m Maha Touri, a passionate Mechatronics and Robotics Engineering student with a strong focus on autonomous systems, embedded programming, and intelligent control. From a young age, I loved disassembling things in my dad’s workshop, fascinated by how they worked. That early curiosity blossomed into a deep desire to become an engineer. My interdisciplinary background spans mechanical, electrical, and software engineering, enabling me to design and develop real-world robotic systems that seamlessly integrate mechanical structures, sensor-driven logic, and embedded control.
